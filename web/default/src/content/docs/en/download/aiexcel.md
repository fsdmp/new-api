## Installation Guide

![Installation Guide](/docs/images/aiexcel/install-guide.png)

### 1. Download the Installer

Choose the installer for your operating system:

**Windows:**

[Download Windows Installer](https://download.jsunpack.tech/aiexcel/aiexcel-setup-win.exe)

**macOS:**

[Download macOS Installer](https://download.jsunpack.tech/aiexcel/aiexcel-setup-mac.dmg)

### 2. Install and Launch

Run the downloaded installer and follow the prompts to complete the installation.

### 3. Click "Install to Excel"

Click the blue "Install to Excel" button in the application interface.

![Install Button](/docs/images/aiexcel/install-button.png)

### 4. Restart Microsoft Excel

**Completely quit** Excel (not just close the workbook), then reopen it.

> **Tip:** On macOS, use `Cmd + Q` to fully quit. On Windows, right-click the taskbar icon and select "Close window."

### 5. Start Using

1. Open Excel and find the "Home" tab in the ribbon
2. Click the "Claude 中文版" button
3. The AI panel will appear on the right side — start typing your questions

![Excel Usage](/docs/images/aiexcel/excel-usage.png)

## FAQ

### Can't see the plugin button in Excel after installation?

Make sure you have **completely quit and restarted Excel**, not just closed the workbook. If the button still doesn't appear, try restarting your computer.

### macOS says "cannot be opened because the developer cannot be verified"?

1. Go to "System Settings → Privacy & Security"
2. Find the blocked app at the bottom and click "Open Anyway"
3. Alternatively, right-click the app icon and select "Open"

### AI responses are slow?

AI response speed depends on network conditions and model load. Complex questions may take 10-30 seconds. If there is no response for an extended period, try resending your question.

### Does it support WPS?

WPS is not supported. Only **Microsoft Excel** (Microsoft 365 or Office 2021+) is supported.

### Is my data safe?

Your data is **encrypted and transmitted** through a local gateway to the AI API and cannot be intercepted by third parties. Excel file contents are only processed during the conversation and are not stored permanently.
